The first batch of the vaccine created by Pfizer and the German company BioNTech arrived in the U.K. last week. Doses are scheduled to be delivered to hospitals beginning Monday. Nursing home residents, health care workers and people age 80 and over will be first in line. Across England, 50 hospitals have been chosen as hubs to administer the vaccine, the BBC reported. The government has initially ordered 40 million doses, which will vaccinate 20 million people. The National Health Service has said that more hubs will be set up as more doses arrive.

Pre-planning a funeral provides an opportunity to identify the many funeral options available to you prior to the time of need. Pre-paying a funeral ensures there is money set aside when the time comes. You do not need to pre-pay if you pre-plan a funeral.
